Interests

Food

I love to eat all kinds of food. I don't really have a favorite food because my cravings change every day. As long as it tastes good, and it's not poisonous, I'll eat it.


Classic Video Games

Video games were a lot simpler when I was younger. Graphics from the 80s and 90s made sense and the adventures had simple yet challenging goals. I would also give my little brother the broken controller so he could learn valuable life-lessons while I jump-kicked his character into oblivion. Good times.


Good Bad Movies

If Mystery Science Theater 3000 has taught me anything, it's you can find entertainment in awful movies as long as you have friends and/or robots to add hilarious commentary to the experience. Examples of good bad movies I've enjoyed in recent years include The Room, Samurai Cop, and Miami Connection. God, they're terrible.


Word Games

Games, particularly board games, that rely on word-skills and quick thinking are what I enjoy the most. One minute I'm using intellectual words with four syllables and the next I'm repetitively saying "um," "you know," and "why are you on my team?"
